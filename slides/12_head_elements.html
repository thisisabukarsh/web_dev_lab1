Head Elements

The unseen but essential part of your webpage

What goes in the <head>?

The <head> section contains metadata about the document, not visible on the page itself but crucial for browsers and search engines.

<title>

Document title.

<title>My Site</title>

<meta>

Metadata (charset, viewport).

<meta charset="UTF-8">

<link>

External resources (CSS, Fonts).

<link rel="stylesheet" href="style.css">

Google Fonts

Integrate custom fonts.

<link href="...">

The <head> section is vital for SEO, responsiveness, and external resource loading
